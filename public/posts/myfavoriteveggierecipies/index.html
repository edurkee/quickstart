Some of my favorite vegetarian recipies

Butternut Squash Risotto

  • Vegan (swap out the butter)
  • total time: ~45 min
  • Leftovers are insane
  • Roasting the squash can take a minute
  • link: https://steffaniebusseyathome.com/home/butternut-squash-risotto/

///

Balsamic Tofu

  • Vegan
  • pretty quick, ~25 min
  • calls for a bunch of herbs and stuff for the marinade/sauce which is kind of a pain. really tasty though.
  • link: https://dishingouthealth.com/balsamic-tofu-20-minutes/

Farro Salad

  • Vegan
  • Farro, tomatoes, those little balls of cheese, artichoke hearts, basil, evoo, and whatever else your heart desires.
  • mix it all together.
